About Me
I design and study reasoning-centric AI systems that can understand, reason, and justify their decisions. My research focuses on improving the faithfulness, interpretability, and reliability of Large Language Models (LLMs) through structured reasoning, attribution, and reinforcement learning.

I also explore applications in code intelligence and software security, where I combine graph learning and LLMs to build systems for vulnerability detection, reasoning, and automated repair.

My broader goal is to develop trustworthy AI systems that can collaborate effectively with humans in high-stakes environments. Learn more →
